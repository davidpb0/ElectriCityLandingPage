ElectriCity

Find electric car charging and bicing stations

Features

  • Find electric car charging and bicing stations
  • View real-time availability and charger type for charging stations and bicing locations
  • Earn rewards for using sustainable transportation options

How it works

1. Download the ElectriCity app

2. Sign up for an account

3. Find a charging station or bicing location using the app's map feature

4. Reserve for your charging session or found a bicing station through the app

5. Enjoy your ride and earn rewards for using sustainable transportation!
